OUR STORY

At Salon Urembo, we believe that beauty is not just about appearance; it's about feeling good inside and out. Founded in 2006, our salon has transformed into a one-stop sanctuary where clients can indulge in a range of beauty treatments designed to enhance their natural beauty. Our team of passionate professionals is dedicated to providing exceptional service and personalised care.

What started as a simple idea has blossomed into a thriving community hub for beauty enthusiasts, where we celebrate individuality and self-expression.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Salon Urembo is to empower our clients by enhancing their beauty and confidence. We strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone feels valued and beautiful. We are committed to using high-quality products and the latest techniques to deliver exceptional results.

MEET OUR CEO: LYDIA

Lydia has been at the helm of Salon Urembo since its birth. With multiple decades worth of experience under her belt, she brings unparalleled leadership and insight to the business. Lydia is committed to fostering a culture of innovation and growth in the cosmetic industry whilst ensuring that Salon Urembo remains at the forefront of beauty.
